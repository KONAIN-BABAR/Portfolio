Mess Management System

The Meal_Log table is used to keep track of which student received which meal on a specific date. It records details such as the student ID, the meal ID, the date the meal was served, and a confirmation (like a signature) that the student actually received the meal. This table acts like a digital signature book or attendance sheet for meals, helping management ensure that meals are distributed properly and are not misused or claimed multiple times. On the other hand, the Audit_Log table is designed for tracking and security purposes. It records every significant change made to the database, such as inserting, updating, or deleting records. This includes information about which user performed the change, on which table, at what time, and which specific record was affected.
